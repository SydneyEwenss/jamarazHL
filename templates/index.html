Welcome to the Ricky Jamaraz Higher or Lower Game!

In this game, two songs will be displayed, and you have to guess which one is more popular based on its Spotify popularity score.

Each correct guess earns you a point! The game ends when you make an incorrect guess. Try to get as many correct as you can and beat the high score.
